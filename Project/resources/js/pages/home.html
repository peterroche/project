Raspberry Pi

The project uses a Raspberry Pi 2, breadboard and a DS18B20 temperature sensor. The GPIO pins on the Pi are wired to the breadboard and in turn to the temperature sensor via a resistor. The DS18B20 is a 1-wire temperature sensor with an accuracy of around 0.5°C over most of it's range.
